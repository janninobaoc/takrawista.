Sepak Takraw Never Stop

Sepak Takraw is a foot volleyball game where players touch as well as handle the ball using only their feet, knee, chest and head.A very popular game around Southeast Asia, the game is played using a rattan or synthetic ball. A sport that appears to be tough to perform, but one that may provide you with a great deal of satisfaction if you can become interested in it.

Our Thoughts

We are more than teammates, we are brothers. We are completely there for each other. We sweat and blead for each other on the court.

Every time I played this sport, I felt completely satisfied; I made numerous sacrifices in this sport, and all of this are well worth it because it allowed me to have fun and meet new people. This sport also allows me to improve my own talents and boost my self-confidence which is very useful for me during this day. For me, nothing can match the pleasure this sport has given me. Furthermore, I have traveled to various locations in Cebu Province as a result of this sport, and I am grateful that I am one of the so-called takrawista who offers his heart in this sport.
