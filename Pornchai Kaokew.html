Pornchai Kaokew

Name: Pornchai Kaokew
Nickname: "Left-footed Killer"
Country: Thailand
Height: 5'8
Position: Killer / Striker
Weight: 68kg
Born:August 24, 1981

Pornchai, 33, is a former soccer player and a soldier groomed by Thailand's army to maintain the country's tight stranglehold on a sport few outside of Asia have even heard of.
